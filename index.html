Champions League final: Manchester City vs Chelsea

The match will take place at the Ataturk Olympic Stadium in Istanbul on May 29, 2021. The venue was down to host the 2020 final until that was postponed by the coronavirus pandemic. This will be the second time the Champions League final will be hosted in Istanbul after Liverpool's famous comeback victory over AC Milan in 2005, held at the same ground.
